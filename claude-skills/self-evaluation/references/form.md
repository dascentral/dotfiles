# Self Reflection Form

## What contributions and impact would you like to share?

>Consider your goals, key results, and how you have demonstrated our values.

## In what ways have you demonstrated our values?

>Where possible, provide examples of: Invention, Bravery, Elevation, Generosity.


## In what parts of your role are you performing best?

>Consider role expectations and strengths.


## In what parts of your role can you improve or grow?

>Consider role expectations and development areas.


## What are your goals for career growth and development?

>Highlight any actions you would propose to learn, grow and advance.


## Overall, how would you evaluate your own performance?

>Consider both craft skills and expected behaviors. Compare and discuss with your manager.

1. Missing the Mark - Inconsistent performance and outcomes that are impacting the teams and the work. Requires a clear shift toward a more steady and dependable level of contribution.
2. On Track - Consistently delivers to a high standard and provides valued contributions to the team. Focused on opportunities to grow and demonstrate mastery of the role.
3. Setting the Pace - Regularly exceeds expectations by delivering high-impact work and demonstrating advanced capabilities. Sets a high bar and serves as a role model for others.
4. Trailblazing - Significantly outperforms expectations and elevates our teams. Inspires others by pushing boundaries to shape the future and deliver transformational impact.
